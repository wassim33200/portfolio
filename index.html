About Me

Hi, I'm Wassim Hamdi, a Data Scientist and final-year Statistics and Data Science student based in Algeria. My journey into data began with a love for statistics and programming, which quickly grew into a deeper passion for machine learning, artificial intelligence, and solving real-world problems with data.

I thrive on uncovering patterns in complex datasets and building models that make a measurable impact. From predictive analytics to algorithm development, I believe in using data ethically and effectively to drive smart decisions and innovation.

I'm proficient in Python, R, SQL, and well-versed in machine learning and deep learning frameworks. I also enjoy building video games using Unity and C#, a creative outlet that sharpens both my logic and problem-solving skills.
